The Ministik Lake
Game Bird Sanctuary

Want to take a walk on the wild side?

The "Ministik" (for short) is a largely unmaintained area of public land about 24 km east of Edmonton, Alberta. Choose from short, liesurely hikes on roads to day-long excursions that may test your stamina and route-finding abilities. You'll find all the options here.

If you're new to the Ministik, click on the Start Here link below to learn more about the area and for suggested starter hikes.
